Hashira - Kimetsu no Yaiba

Hashira is the highest rank that Demon Slayers can obtain in the Demon Slayer Corps. Those who hold the position are regarded as the most powerful and skilled combatants in the entire organization, protecting humanity against more dangerous threats that cannot be handled by other Demon Slayers.

gyomei himejima

Gyomei is a hulking figure among the Demon Slayers. He is one of the tallest characters in the series, easily towering over his fellow Hashira.

Yoriichi Kamado

Yoriichi Tsugikuni was a Demon Slayer who lived during the Sengoku era, almost 500 years before the main events.

Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko is a kind and caring girl that takes care of her family, often helping with chores around the home.

Obanai Iguro

Obanai is a 21-year-old young man of short stature and a pale complexion.
